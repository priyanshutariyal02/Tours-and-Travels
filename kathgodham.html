KATHGODHAM TO ADI-KAILASH & OM PARVAT
YATRA

Day-1

Kathgodam - Patal Bhubaneswar

- Receiving in Kathgodam.
- Breakfast in Kainchi Dham.
- Kanichi Dham mandir darshan( Neem Karoli Baba).
- Lunch in Jageshwar.
- Jageshwar Dham Mandir darshan Almora.
- Stay in Patal Bhubaneswar.

Day-2

Patal Bhubaneswar - Dharchula

- Breakfast in Patal Bhubaneswar.
- Patal Bhubaneswar Mandir darshan.
- Travel to Dharchula 5hour approx.
- Medical and permit formalities and museum and nepal visit.
- Medical check up and permit formalities.
- Visit to Rung Museum and Nepal.

Day-3

Dharchula - Nabi Village

- Breakfast in Dharchula.
- Dharchula to Nabi Village.
- Briefing in TV Tower.
- Lunch in Budi Village.
- Enroute Sightseeing- Thakti , Pelsitha waterfall and chiyalekh.
- Dinner and stay in Nabi.

Day-4

Nabi - Om Parvat then Kuti Village

- Breakfast in Nabi Village.
- Manokamna Mandir visit in Nabi.
- Kalapani visit (origin of Kali Nadi), Vyas Gufa sightseeing.
- Lunch in the Nabi Village.
- Om Parvat visit.
- Nabi to Kuti( Dinner and stay in Kuti).

Day-5

Gunji - Kuti - Jyolingkong then back to Kuti

- Breakfast in Kuti.
- Adi Kailash Darshan.
- Trek to Gauri kund and Parvati Kund (5-6km).
- Lunch in Jyolingkong.
- Sightseeing - Bhrama Parvat, Ganesh Nala, Pandav Parvat, Parvati Mukut.
- Back to Kuti (Dinner and stay in Kuti).

Day-6

Kuti - Dharchula

- Breakfast in Kuti.
- Lunch in Budi.
- Dinner & stay in Dharchula.

Day-7

Back - Kathgodham

- Breakfast in Dharchula.
- Lunch in Dhaniya.
- Drop at Kathgodam.
