DHARCHULA TO ADI-KAILASH & OM PARVAT
YATRA

Day-1

Dharchula 940m

After arriving at Dharchula go to the tourist rest house, and after taking bath, take your breakfast.
Then give us your paper for the Inner line Permit and Medical.
After Lunch, Day is free for outings, to Dharchula and Nepal.
Then move to the Tourist Rest House and take your dinner. Stay there for the whole night.
Next day we will receive you.

Day-2

Dharchula - Gunji (71 KMs/ 2.5 hrs) 3200m

After taking bath, we take our breakfast and then we start to drive to Gunji.
Gunji village is the Front Gate for Adi Kailash and OM Parvat.
Between Crossing the village(Gunji) we pass these routes.
1. Chialekh
2. Garbyang
3. Nepalchu
-Between the route, we take our lunch.
-In the evening, we reached Gunji.
-Then we move to the Tourist Rest House and take our dinner.

Day-3

Gunji - Adi Kailash - Gunji (68 KMs) 4497m

After taking bath, we start the Drive from Gunji to Joling kong.
After Reaching Joling kong, then we move towards Adi Kailash Temple.
Then we Perform Pooja at Lord Shiva & Goddess Parvati Temple near Parvati Sarovar.
After Lunch, we drive back to Gunji.
Between the route we see:
1. Brahma Parvat
2. Pandava Parvat
3. Pandava Fort
4. Parvati Mukut
In the evening, we reached Gunji.
Then we move to the Tourist Rest House for dinner, and also, stay there the whole night.

Day-4

Gunji - OM Parvat - Gunji / Nabi (22 Kms ) 4246m

After taking bath, we start the Drive from Gunji to Om Parvat.
Reaching Om Parvat we Meditate.
Between the route we see:
1. Kali Mandir (Kala Pani)
2. SheshNag Parvat
3. Cave of Ved Vyas
Between the route, we take our lunch.
Then we drive to return back to Gunji / Nabi.
Then we move to the Tourist Rest House for dinner, and also, stay there the whole night.

Day-5

Gunji / Nabi - Dharchula (69KMs ) 3200m

After taking bath, we take our breakfast and then we start to drive from Gunji to Dharchula.
After reaching there, then we take Lunch.
Then we drop you at Dharchula Tourist rest house.
